Tilant history and its fashion

Tilant is Arab largest fashion retailer, one of the biggest retail conglomerates in the middle East . Founded in 1996 as a single store in peninsula Arab island, in the Emirates, Tilant has grown to over 70 store and 30 brand stores across 7 countries in the lebnan, Palestine, Qater, Syria and aljeez.
